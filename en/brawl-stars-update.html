🔔 Brawl Stars Update 2026

Update 66 – Prestige · February/March 2026 · Brawl Mystery updated

The February 2026 Update (Update 66 – "Prestige") is one of the biggest in Brawl Stars history. Alongside two new Brawlers, the entire trophy and matchmaking system gets a complete overhaul. Here's everything at a glance – and what it means for Brawl Mystery.

📣 Brawl Mystery Update: Sirius and Najia have been added to the quiz. The Brawler Library is up to date. All new attributes are reflected in Classic Mode.

Changelog – All Major Changes

New Brawlers

NEWSirius – 100th Brawler (Ultra Legendary)

The historic milestone Brawler. Sirius summons the shadows of defeated enemies and sends them back into the fight. Unlockable via the "Sirius Release Challenge" (own all 99 other Brawlers + win 1 match each) or via Brawler Boxes.

New Brawlers

NEWNajia – 101st Brawler (Mythic), March 2026

Part of the "Pyramid Quest" theme. Najia fights with Egyptian-inspired snakes and stacking poison damage. Especially effective against Tanks and in zone-based game modes.

Trophy System

REWORKEDNew Prestige System

The seasonal Trophy reset system is replaced by Brawler Prestige. Reach 1,000 trophies with a Brawler once and they're stored permanently – no more resets! Prestige Ranks 1–3 grant exclusive cosmetics and a unique Neon Brawler title at Prestige 3.

Matchmaking

REWORKEDHidden MMR System

Matchmaking now uses a hidden MMR value that measures real skill – independent of trophy count. High-skill players now face opponents of similar ability more often. Names are displayed as "?" from Prestige 2 onwards.

Game System

NEWRush Events

Time-limited Rush Events offer bonus rewards for wins. Players activate Rush Mode manually before joining a match. Rewards include: Coins, Power Points, Chaos Drops, Starr Drops and bonus trophies.

Game Mode

NEWLoaded Showdown

A battle royale mode with power-ups, weapons and vehicles. Adds more variety to the classic Showdown format and appeals to a broader player base.

Brawler System

REWORKEDBuffies Expansion

Mythic Gears are removed (players receive full compensation). Instead, Buffies for classic Brawlers are strengthened. A new claw machine lets you win Buffies – the first attempt each day is free.

Brawl Pass

NEWNew Brawl Pass Seasons 2026

Season 48 (March 2026): "Sands of Time" – desert-themed content to match Najia. Season 49 (April 2026): "Dragons and Fairies" – a fairy-tale fantasy theme.

Brawler Balance

CHANGEDCrow & Glowy Overhaul

Crow gets two new Gadgets: "Instapoison" (instantly trigger all poison damage + shield) and "Slowing Toxin" (kunai slows and poisons). Glowy's Super during Hypercharge is now 360 degrees with 20% increased damage taken for targets.

What this means for Brawl Mystery

With the major update, all new and changed Brawlers have been added to the Brawl Mystery quiz:

  • Sirius is now available as a daily puzzle (Ultra Legendary, 2026, Controller)
  • Najia will appear as a daily puzzle from March 2026 (Mythic, 2026, Damage Dealer)
  • The Brawler Library has been expanded to include both Brawlers
  • Buffie Brawlers like Crow may have slightly updated attributes
💡 Advantage for Quick Players: Players who know the new Brawlers have a head start in Brawl Mystery – as many players are still unfamiliar with Sirius and Najia. Learn their attributes now!
