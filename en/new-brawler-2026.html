✨ New Brawlers 2026 – Sirius & Najia

As of: March 2026 · February Update · Brawl Mystery already updated

The February 2026 Update was historic: with Sirius, the 100th Brawler steps onto the field – and he's Ultra Legendary. Shortly after, Najia follows as the 101st Brawler (Mythic). Brawl Mystery has already added both to the daily quiz. Here's everything you need to recognise them fast.

⭐ Ultra Legendary · #100 · February 2026

Sirius – The Shadow Tamer

Sirius – Abilities & Playstyle

Sirius is the second Ultra Legendary Brawler in the game (after Kaze) and introduces a completely new mechanic: collecting and commanding shadows.

⚔️ Attack: Binary Starr

Sirius conjures two projectiles simultaneously: a "ticket bomb" for area damage and a "shadow strike" for single-target hits. The combination makes him effective against both groups and individual targets.

🌑 Super: Shadow Summon

Sirius unleashes all collected Brawler shadows. These inherit the traits of their original Brawler – attack style, health and abilities. Up to 3 shadows active at once.

⚡ Hypercharge: Unlimited Power!

During Hypercharge, summoned shadows have increased damage and health – making a full 3-shadow deployment the most powerful team-reversal tool in the game.

🌟 Star Powers

Dusk Runners: Shadows move faster. The Darkest Starr: Every 4th hit with Shadow Strike stores 2 shadows instead of 1 – significantly faster Super charging.

Recognising Sirius in Brawl Mystery

  • Rarity: Ultra Legendary (only 2 Brawlers in this category!)
  • Release Year: 2026
  • Role: Controller
  • Colour (Pixel Mode): Dark violet/black with star-shaped highlights
  • Emoji Mode: Expect emojis like 👥, 🌑, ⭐ or ✨
  • Description: "shadows", "control", "summon", "100th Brawler"
💡 Quiz Tip: In Classic Mode: when "Ultra Legendary" lights up green, it's either Kaze or Sirius. Release year decides the rest – Kaze = 2024, Sirius = 2026.
✦ Mythic · #101 · March 2026

Najia – The Serpent Keeper

Najia – Abilities & Playstyle

Najia is the 101st Brawler and brings stacking poison to the battlefield. She fits the "Pyramid Quest" theme of Brawl Stars 2026 and fights with Egyptian-inspired snake attacks.

🐍 Attack: SSSNAKE DELIVERY

Najia throws a vessel containing a snake. During flight, the snake can be released in any direction. The vessel deals direct damage; the snake deals stacking poison over time.

🏺 Super: DANGER NOODLES

Releases 3 poison snakes onto the field, which control areas or can be repositioned inside urns. Creates sustained denial pressure on key positions.

🌟 Star Powers

Venomous Guardian: Brawlers defeated by snake poison spawn a new snake. Toxic: Poison damage scales with the target's health – especially strong against Tanks.

Recognising Najia in Brawl Mystery

  • Rarity: Mythic
  • Release Year: 2026
  • Role: Damage Dealer
  • Colour (Pixel Mode): Sandy/beige with golden accents, snake details
  • Emoji Mode: 🐍, 🏺, ☠️ or ⚗️
  • Description: "snakes", "poison", "vases", "pyramid"
