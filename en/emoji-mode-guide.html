😊 Emoji Mode – The Complete Guide

Strategy · Tips · Common Mistakes · Example Puzzles · Win Faster

The Emoji Mode is the most creative and cryptic mode in Brawl Mystery. Three emojis describe a Brawler – but you only see the first one at the start. Each wrong guess reveals another. Sounds simple? It's not. This guide shows you how to crack the emoji language of Brawl Mystery.

How does Emoji Mode work?

At the start you see one single emoji. If you guess wrong, the second emoji appears. After another wrong guess the third arrives. You have 8 attempts in total. The earlier you identify the Brawler, the better your score.

Emojis are carefully chosen and relate to the Brawler's appearance, personality, weapons, or backstory. There's no randomness – every emoji combination has logic!

Strategy: Understanding the emoji language

Category 1 – Weapons & Combat Style

Many emojis refer directly to the Brawler's attack:

  • 🔫 → Firearm (Colt, Brock, Rico…)
  • 💣 → Bombs/Explosions (Dynamike, Tick, Barley…)
  • ⛏️ → Pickaxe or tool (Jacky, Carl…)
  • 🥊 → Melee / Fists (El Primo, Bull…)
  • 🪄 → Magic (Gene, Tara, Sprout…)
  • 🏹 → Arrow/Ranged (Bo, Nita…)
  • 🐍 → Snakes / Poison (Crow, Najia…)

Category 2 – Personality & Character

  • 😈 → Villain / dark personality (Mortis, Crow, Edgar…)
  • 😊 / 🌟 → Friendly, positive (Poco, Leon, Sandy…)
  • 😡 / 💢 → Aggressive, angry (El Primo, Frank, Ash…)
  • 🤖 → Robot / Mechanical (8-Bit, Surge, Nani…)
  • 👻 → Ghost / Supernatural (Mortis, Squeak…)

Category 3 – Appearance & Style

  • 🎩 → Hat (Mr. P, Chester…)
  • 🌵 → Cactus / Nature (Spike, Sprout…)
  • 🦅 / 🐦 → Birds / Wings (Crow, Bo…)
  • 🐻 / 🐾 → Animal design (Nita with bear, Leon as lion…)
  • 👑 → Royal / Noble (Gene, Amber…)
  • 🌊 → Water / Surfing (Buzz, Max, Sandy…)

Category 4 – Background & Profession

  • 🏴‍☠️ → Pirate theme (Darryl, Penny…)
  • 🎸 → Music (Poco, Emz…)
  • 🔬 → Science / Inventor (Jessie, Penny, Sprout…)
  • 🍺 → Bartender/Drinks (Barley, Gus…)
  • ⛏️ + 💎 → Miner/Mine (Carl, Jacky…)
💡 Pro Tip: Always think in combinations. A single emoji like 🔫 could match many Brawlers – but 🔫 + 🤠 = almost certainly Colt or a sheriff-themed Brawler. The combination makes all the difference!

Tips for faster solutions

  • Categorize the first emoji immediately: Is it a weapon, personality, or appearance? This narrows the group
  • Think through the full Brawler list: Which Brawlers fit the emoji category? List them mentally
  • Learn combinations: Play Brawl Stars actively and internalize character themes
  • Rare emojis = rare Brawler? Very specific emojis often point to Mythic/Legendary
  • Factor in new Brawlers: Sirius 2026 → 👥🌑⚡ (shadows, darkness, power)

Common mistakes in Emoji Mode

  • ❌ Thinking too literally: 🌹 doesn't always mean "flower" – it can mean charm or elegance
  • ❌ Rushing on the first emoji: Wait until you're sure – one more emoji can reveal a lot
  • ❌ Confusing similar Brawler themes: Multiple Brawlers share similar emoji themes (e.g. all "evil" Brawlers)
  • ❌ Waiting too long: If you have no idea after 2 emojis, guess your best bet – more hints arrive faster

Example puzzles – Emoji Mode

The hidden Brawler is Barley (Super Rare, Artillery, the bartender who throws bottles):

  • Emoji 1: 🍾 → Bottle! → Thought: Barley, Gus, Amber? Barley throws bottles, so start with him
  • Guess 1: Barley → ✅ Win! One emoji, one guess – possible when you know your Brawlers

Harder example: Hidden Brawler is Mortis:

  • Emoji 1: 🦇 → Bat! → Mortis, right? But what if it's another dark Brawler?
  • Guess 1: Mortis → ✅ Instantly correct! 🦇 = Mortis is one of the clearest emoji assignments
🏆 Goal: Try to solve the Brawler at the first or second emoji. This requires deep Brawl Stars knowledge – but after 2–3 weeks of daily play you'll assign most emojis instantly.
