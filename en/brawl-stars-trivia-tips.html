🧩 Brawl Stars Trivia Tips

Recognize Brawlers Faster · 25 Memory Aids · For All 4 Modes

Want to get faster and better at the Brawl Mystery quiz? The key is trivia knowledge – small, specific facts about each Brawler that help you identify them within seconds. Here are 25 of the most useful Brawl Stars trivia facts for all 4 game modes.

Why trivia matters so much

In Brawl Mystery, one detail often decides everything: a Brawler's color, their release year, a keyword in their description, or a distinctive emoji. Players who know their trivia can be right after the very first hint – those who don't will fight until the last attempt.

Trivia: Rarities & History

Fact #1

Shelly is the only Starting Brawler – she's completely alone in her own rarity tier. In Classic Mode she's always the perfect first diagnostic guess.

Fact #2

There are currently 2 Ultra Legendary Brawlers: Kaze (2024) and Sirius (Feb. 2026). When the target Brawler shows "Ultra Legendary", your choice is minimal!

Fact #3

Sirius is the 100th Brawler – a historic milestone from February 2026. Najia is the 101st Brawler (Mythic, March 2026).

Fact #4

The first 9 Brawlers in the game (2017) are: Shelly, Colt, Bull, Brock, Dynamike, El Primo, Barley, Poco, Jessie. All are Rare or Super Rare – not a single Epic among them!

Fact #5

Nearly all Legendary Brawlers have long range or unique mechanics. Exceptions: Meg (Tank/Medium) and Chester (Medium range).

Trivia: Colors & Appearance

Fact #6

Leon is the only Brawler with a distinctive blue-green hair mane. In Pixel Mode: if you see blue streaks, think Leon!

Fact #7

Mr. P is the only Brawler with a top hat and penguin design. In Pixel Mode: black top hat + white belly = Mr. P.

Fact #8

Spike is the only cactus Brawler – his body is completely green with spines. Unmistakable in Pixel Mode.

Fact #9

Amber is the only Brawler with flaming hair – orange fire tongues on her head, combined with a drinking bottle as weapon.

Fact #10

Sirius (2026) wears a black-purple costume with star-shaped elements. As a "shadow Brawler" his color palette is nearly unique in the roster.

Trivia: Roles & Playstyle

Fact #11

There are only 2 Artillery Brawlers in the Rare class: Barley and Dynamike. If the arrow confirms Artillery and Rare, the choice is easy.

Fact #12

All Marksman Brawlers have Long or Very Long range. Exception: Bea has medium range due to her unique playstyle.

Fact #13

Only Tank Brawlers have "Very High" health. Tanks include: Bull, El Primo, Frank, Rosa, Jacky, Ash, Buzz and a few others.

Fact #14

Mortis is the fastest Brawler in the game – he has "Very Fast" speed. In Classic Mode: "Very Fast" + Assassin = Mortis (almost certain).

Fact #15

Most Assassin Brawlers are Mythic. Edgar (Epic) and Leon (Legendary) are the exceptions below and above.

Trivia: Emoji Mode Memory Aids

Fact #16

Mortis = 🦇 – the bat is his definitive signature as a vampire-inspired Brawler.

Fact #17

Gene = 🏮 or 🪄 – the magic lantern is his most important symbol. His Super pulls enemies like a genie from a lamp.

Fact #18

Sandy = 💤 (sleep emoji) – Sandy sleeps during combat (lore!) and produces his sleeping sand. Almost always the first or second emoji.

Fact #19

Poco = 🎸 – he's a guitarist and bard. The music emoji is almost always present with Poco.

Fact #20

Crow = 🐦‍⬛ (black bird / crow) – the name says it all. Combined with 🐍 (poison) almost always unambiguous.

Trivia: Description Mode

Fact #21

Only Jessie and Pam are mother and daughter – if one mentions the other in a description, you instantly know the Brawler.

Fact #22

Barley is the only "former bartender" in the Brawl Stars universe. The word "bartender" or "lost" + "drinks" = Barley.

Fact #23

Sprout was created by Jessie and Penny – his description mentions robotics and plant mechanics. Combined, those are clear keywords.

Fact #24

El Primo is a professional wrestler – his real name and wrestling career appear in his description. "Ring", "champion", "mask" = El Primo.

Fact #25

Najia (2026) is part of the "Pyramid Quest" theme – her description revolves around snakes, vases and Egyptian-inspired elements. Unique in the current roster.
